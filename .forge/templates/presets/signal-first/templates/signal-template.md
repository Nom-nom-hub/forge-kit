# Signal Template (Signal-First Preset)

Capture observations, anomalies, and feedback signals as they emerge.

## Fields

- **id**: `sig-{8-char-nanoid}` — auto-generated
- **source**: Where the signal came from (user, event, system, research, audit)
- **severity**: observation, concern, warning, critical
- **category**: ux, performance, security, reliability, process, business, technical, other
- **description**: What was observed (1-3 sentences)
- **context**: When, where, and how it was observed
